# UncleCode Korean Demo Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Baemin DoHyeon-inspired Korean product motion: chunky, cheerful, direct, and terminal-smart. The system should feel like a serious developer tool translated into punchy Korean poster language, with enough grid discipline to keep it technical.

## Colors

- Background: `#fff7d6`
- Ink: `#161616`
- Deep teal: `#00a39b`
- Command blue: `#0057ff`
- Punch red: `#ef3e2f`
- Terminal green: `#09a66d`

## Typography

- Korean display: local `Do Hyeon` from `assets/fonts/DoHyeon-Regular.ttf`
- Code and command text: `IBM Plex Mono`, fallback `Courier New`, monospace

## Motion Rules

- Korean headlines should pop in like poster blocks, not fade politely.
- Scene changes use block wipes and quick light flashes.
- Terminal details stay crisp, but the overall rhythm can feel more playful than the English Swiss version.

## What NOT To Do

- No generic dark AI dashboard look.
- No purple gradients, glow blobs, or glass cards.
- No tiny Korean copy; Hangul needs room at video scale.
- No jump cuts.
